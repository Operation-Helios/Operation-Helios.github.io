Welcome to Operation Helios!

We are a team of two students based in Brisbane, Australia who participate in the hobby of High Altitude Ballooning. With our objective to capture footage from the stratosphere, we began working on this project in mid 2010, and after resolving many complex and unexpected obstacles, we launched a balloon in April 2012. We plan to launch again in 2013, with a significantly improved camera system to capture panoramic video.
